About FocusTodo

FocusTodo is designed around one principle: simplicity. No complex features or overwhelming options. Just pure focus on what matters most - getting things done.

How to Use

  • Tap the + button to add a new task
  • Tap a task to mark it as complete
  • Swipe right to complete a task
  • Swipe left to delete a task
  • Long press to delete a task

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create an account?
A: On the sign-in screen, tap "Don't have an account? Sign up" to create a new account. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
Q: I'm getting a network error when trying to sign in. What should I do?
A: Please check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, try these steps:
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Check if you're behind a firewall or using a VPN
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Update to the latest version of the app
Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, your data is securely stored and encrypted. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.
Q: Can I use FocusTodo on multiple devices?
A: Yes, your tasks sync automatically across all devices where you're signed in with the same account.
Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Currently, password reset functionality is being implemented. Please contact support for assistance.
